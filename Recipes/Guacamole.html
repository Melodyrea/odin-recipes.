Delicioso Guacamole

Guacamole is traditionally made by mashing peeled, ripe avocados and sea salt with a molcajete y tejolote (mortar and pestle).

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime juce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • fresh cilantro
  • 2 plum tomatoes
  • Minced Garlic
  • optional (blednded fresh jalapeno)

Steps

  1. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl.
  2. Then use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth.
  3. Add the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, garlic, lime juice and salt and stir everything together.
  4. That's it. The most delicious, easy guacamole!
